## Issue R1 — Indonesia GMV Reacceleration

**Materiality:** High
**Classification:** Partially Boundable (score 19/30)
**Underlying FMs:** FM 3 (Indonesia GMV Structural Deceleration), FM 15 (Indonesia/Philippines Correlated Macro Shock)

### Perimeter [score 3/5]

Brazil combined; excludes Monee, Garena. Dependencies: ad take-rate is the **primary
compounding EBITDA driver** in the returns model — its compression affects group EBITDA
margin, not just revenue. Systemic — single-mechanism risk but with asymmetric EBITDA
sensitivity; this is structurally different from other FMs which have GMV exposure
but diluted margin impact.

persists through hold and beyond. Peak period FY2029E (exit-year). Reversibility —
not reversible if structural; reversible if temporary category-mix issue. Recurring

**Timing characterization is fuzzy by design** — this failure mode has no single
