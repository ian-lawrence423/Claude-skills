# Sector-Specific DDR Add-On Modules

Add these sections to the core DDR when the company operates in the relevant vertical. Insert after Section 5 (Product) and before Section 7 (Customer Success), or wherever they fit the deal narrative best.

---

## Fintech / Payments / Fraud & Risk

**Section: Fraud, Risk, and Compliance**

- Describe the fraud/risk models in production — what signals are used, how models are trained, and how often they are retrained.
- Provide false positive and false negative rates over time and describe the tradeoff curve customers can configure.
- Describe how chargebacks, disputes, and liability are allocated between the company, the customer, and the end consumer.
- Provide a breakdown of fraud-related losses or guarantee payouts (if applicable) over the last 3 years.
- Describe the regulatory framework the product operates within (PCI-DSS, SOC2, state money transmission licenses, CFPB, etc.) and any open enforcement actions or audits.
- Describe how model performance degrades across new customer types, geographies, or fraud vectors, and how the company adapts.

---

## Logistics / Supply Chain / Carrier

**Section: Carrier and Logistics Network**

- Provide a breakdown of shipment volume by carrier (domestic and international) and describe concentration risk.
- Describe the nature of carrier relationships — are they contractual integrations, API partnerships, or data feeds? Who owns the integration?
- Describe how carrier API/policy changes have historically affected the product and what the mitigation playbook looks like.
- Provide SLA performance metrics (on-time delivery accuracy, tracking event latency, exception detection rates) over the last 12 months.
- Describe how the company handles carrier onboarding and the current coverage map for international geographies.
- Describe any revenue-sharing, referral, or data-licensing arrangements with carriers and the terms of those agreements.

---

## Healthcare / Life Sciences

**Section: Regulatory, Clinical, and Reimbursement**

- Describe the regulatory classification of the product (SaMD, wellness, administrative tool) and any FDA, CE Mark, or equivalent clearances held.
- Provide a timeline of any regulatory submissions, clearances, or audits in the last five years.
- Describe the reimbursement model — is revenue tied to payer contracts, direct pay, or institutional procurement?
- Describe HIPAA/HITECH compliance posture, including BAA coverage, PHI handling, and audit log practices.
- Describe any clinical studies or outcomes data that support product efficacy claims, and how those claims are used in sales.
- Provide a summary of any open legal or regulatory matters involving patient safety, data privacy, or billing practices.

---

## Consumer / Retail / E-Commerce

**Section: Consumer Engagement and Merchandising**

- Describe the consumer-facing touchpoints the platform powers (notifications, tracking pages, returns portal, post-purchase surveys) and the volume of consumer interactions per month.
- Provide opt-in/opt-out rates for communications channels (email, SMS, push) and how engagement rates have trended.
- Describe any direct consumer data collected and how it is used, shared, or monetized.
- Describe the relationship between consumer experience metrics (CSAT, NPS, delivery accuracy) and retailer retention outcomes.
- Describe any network-level consumer data assets and whether cross-retailer consumer identity is maintained, and under what governance.

---

## B2B SaaS / Platform

**Section: Platform and Integration Ecosystem**

- Provide a breakdown of the current integration ecosystem — key commerce platforms, ERPs, WMS, and other systems integrated with, and the volume of customers on each.
- Describe platform dependency risk (e.g., Shopify, Salesforce, SAP) — what happens to the product if a major platform changes its API or partner terms?
- Describe the partner program (ISVs, SIs, agencies) and the % of new business influenced or sourced by partners.
- Describe the API surface area — what is exposed, what is consumed, and what contractual obligations exist around uptime and versioning.
- Provide a breakdown of customers by integration type and describe whether integration complexity correlates with retention or expansion.
