Because the timing is Unboundable, a standard quarterly monitoring program is structurally inadequate — only vintage-level disclosure or primary research on origination quality can reduce this to Boundable. ## 15. IC Scrutiny Framework — Anticipated Hard Questions

These are the questions a skeptical IC member is most likely to raise on a Sea Ltd thesis.
Pre-answer each before presenting to IC.

**Q1: "The ROIC of 106% looks like a recovery metric — what's the normalized ROIC?"**
At 15–20% GMV growth (normalized), ROIC moderates to an estimated 60–80% [E]. Still well
above any plausible WACC. The 3-year recovery framing should be stated proactively.

**Q2: "The ad take-rate ceiling is hypothesis-level — how do you size the downside?"**
At 2.5% ceiling (plausible at Shopee's AOV), CY2029E EBITDA is ~$1.3B below base.
MOIC impact: −0.33x. IRR falls from 26% to 9–12%. The ceiling cannot be pre-tested —
only monitored. Named Tier 1–2 estimate is required before full-size.

**Q3: "TikTok grew 111% in Indonesia — why isn't this a structural share shift?"**
Three one-time base effects in 2025: (1) Bukalapak exit removing 8–10% of Indonesia GMV
comparison base; (2) Tokopedia promotional rationalization post-merger; (3) TikTok entering
from a low base where growth rates are structurally elevated. Q1 2026 earnings is the first
clean test. If below 10% for a second consecutive quarter, the thesis requires reassessment.

**Q4: "Monee NPL at 1.1% looks fine — what's the actual concern?"**
The concern is not the current level — it's the monitoring lag. At 80%+ YoY loan book growth,
>50% of the book is unseasoned. Aggregate NPL blends seasoned and unseasoned cohorts. A
deteriorating portfolio can show stable aggregate NPL for 6–9 months before seasoned-cohort
defaults outpace new origination dilution. Vintage data is the only fix. This is the single
highest-priority data request.

**Q5: "Brazil is structurally loss-making — why isn't it a larger concern?"**
Brazil is a duration risk, not a binary loss. Each year of breakeven deferral beyond Q4 2027
costs ~$120–155M NPV vs. base case — approximately 0.02–0.03x MOIC per year of delay.
It becomes thesis-threatening only if MELI closes both inhibitors (price + speed) before
Shopee FBS scales, which requires MELI to reach 65%+ same-day coverage in São Paulo/Rio
before Shopee electronics GMV share reaches 15%+. Current data does not support this being
imminent.

**Q6: "You're relying on JPM and UBS who both have active Sea coverage — how do you address confirmation bias?"**
Kill Trigger 3 and Info Gap 2 directly address this. Pattern's assessment has the same
structural limitation as the sell-side research it is built on. Independent primary research
(consumer panel + logistics contacts) is required before full-size. This is stated explicitly
in the information state disclosure.
